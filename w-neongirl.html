Neon and girls

Photography , Digital Design

2016

Neon and girls is a hypothetical concept of a magazine about homosexuality among young generations who are living in Shanghai. Always fascinated with the urban planning and the way people interact with the city and culture, I questioned myself how does the taboo culture of LGBTQ in China grows up and how the young community builds up the brand new perceptions of sexual norms to against the conservative ideas.
