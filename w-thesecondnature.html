The Second
Nature

Prints

2017

The Second Nature is an anthology of Chinese ethnic minority literature. It involved nine novels and a poetry collection mostly written by indigenous minorities. All the article are about different ethnic minorities and ordered by the number of the population from the most to the least. China is a multi-national country. It has 55 ethnic minorities which only account for 8.5 percent of the people in China. Over the past 30 years, a variety of social, economic and political forces have pushed them toward assimilation into mainstream Chinese culture. More and more ethnic minorities' young people are not familiar with their own culture......
