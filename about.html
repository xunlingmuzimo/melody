Melody(Xun) Mo

A graphic designer, photographer, and illustrator

I received my master's degree in graphic design from Otis College of Art and Design in August, 2018. Keeping my mind stimulated with everyday endeavors, my recent goal is becoming an art director shortly and bringing a better experience to all the clients through my design! I believe graphic design is a tool that can solve complicated problems by employing simple visual creativities. At the same time, it can deliver unique values and happiness by the designers and the brands while reinterpreting the way people see the world.
