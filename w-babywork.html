Baby Works

Branding , Digital Design

2015

Baby Works is an app designed for children from three to six years old to develop creativity and communication skills. Children can use painting tools on the app to make arts on their favorite food and submit them to parents who will be deciding if the food can be 3D printed or not. The app provides a platform that allows children and parents to have more interactions and connections.
