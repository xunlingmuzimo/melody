Young Designer Manifesto is a project that I first started in the workshop with All The Way To Paris during 2017 summer at Otis College of Art and Design, and further developed at the end of my study in graduate school. The goal is to motivate younger designers to challenge themselves and keep the mind stimulated with future endeavors.
