A Sexual Knowledge test

Digital Design

2018

A Proficient driver road test/A Sexual Knowledge test is a game that creates a playful solution to engage the younger generation with taboo topics around sexuality. In China, the internet term “a proficient driver” implies people who are experts in a certain field. However, this word nowadays ironically describes people who have much wrong knowledge about sex which picked up from pornography and romantic novels. The goal of this game is to prevent the younger generation from risky sexual behavior led by insufficient sexual information and equip them with the power of being confident and respectful.
