WIND

Branding , Photography , UI&UX

2015

WIND is an album branding project. To make the best album design for the client, I went to the Sun Park in Harbin with the musician, where I found tons of inspirations. Accompanied by the soft breeze, which was the theme of the album, we sang, chatted, and danced under the sun. I designed the collection by putting all of the happiness presented at the day. I believe the music carries energy, so does design.
