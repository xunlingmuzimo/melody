Dress Up

Photography , Branding

2016

Dress Up is an album about a love story from a twelve-year-old girl. The little girl was trying to dress up prettily for her first date to get more attention. Every first date for an inexperienced girl is always exciting and memorable. To express this idea, I used mixed elements from the objects of childishness and maturity.
