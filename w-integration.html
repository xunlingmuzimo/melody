The Ethnic
Minorities
in China

Prints

2017

Integration is a book that introduces the cultures and traditions of different Chinese ethnic minorities. In the 20th century, while such a large number of people are going through modernization at such a fast pace, it brought a significant impact on ethnicities and humanity. The goal of this book is to preserve the intangible cultural heritage of Chinese ethnic minorities, the 8.5% population of China, through images and text
