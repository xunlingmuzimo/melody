Intersection:
Wu & Soulages

Branding

2017

The Intersection begins with the work of Guanzhong Wu and Pierre Soulages, master painters—Guanzhong from China, Soulages from France. Both born in 1919, each takes influence and stylistic cues from the culture of the other. The cultural intersection between the two and their expansion and elevation of calligraphic and impressionistic forms have broken formal restraints of the past and inspired an entire movement in contemporary art.
